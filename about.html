Meet Our Captain

Captain Sanford Carter

U.S. Coast Guard Licensed Captain & Owner

With years of experience guiding inshore and offshore trips along the beautiful Georgia coast, Captain Sanford combines deep local knowledge with an unwavering commitment to safety and customer satisfaction. As the owner and operator of Sunbury Charters, he has safely guided hundreds of successful fishing trips annually, building a reputation for professionalism and exceptional service.

  • U.S. Coast Guard Licensed Captain
  • Fully Insured Business Operator
  • CPR & First Aid Certified
  • Specializing in Inshore, Nearshore & Offshore Trips
  • Active in Conservation Efforts & Fishing Tournaments
